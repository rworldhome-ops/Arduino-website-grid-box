Arduino Uno

Ardruino uno is the original arduino, its what comes to mind when you say arduino.

The Arduino Uno Rev3 is a 5V microcontroller board based on the ATmega328P, featuring a 16 MHz clock speed, 32 KB Flash memory, 2 KB SRAM, 14 digital I/O pins (6 PWM), and 6 analog inputs. It supports 7-12V input voltage, 20-40mA DC current per I/O pin, and includes USB, UART, SPI, and I2C interfaces.
