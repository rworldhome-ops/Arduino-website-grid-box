Arduino Nano

The Arduino Nano is small and compact, making it ideal for portable projects and builds where space is limited. It fits easily into breadboards and can control sensors, LEDs, and other electronic components despite its small size.

Key specifications

  • microcontroller: ATmega328P
  • operating voltage: 5V
  • digital I/O pins: 14 (6 support PWM)
  • analog input pins: 8
  • clock speed: 16 MHz
  • flash memory: 32 KB
  • SRAM: 2 KB
  • EEPROM: 1 KB
  • USB connection: mini-USB or micro-USB depending on the version
